How To Earn Pixels (Points)

Earn pixels by watching the stream, chatting, participating in events, and being part of the community.

You get 15 pixels for every 10 minutes of watch time, 1,000 pixels just for following the channel, 1000 every month you subscribe to the channel, 500 for every 100 bits cheered, and 500 for every $1 tipped to the channel. Subscribers also get a x3 multiplier on watchtime.

There are also special Twitch channel point redeems to earn points and random bonuses for special events.

How To Redeem

Click a reward command to copy it, then paste that command into Twitch chat. Make sure you have enough pixels before redeeming.
